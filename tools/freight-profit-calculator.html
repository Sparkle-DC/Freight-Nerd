What the risk score means

The risk score does not predict the future. It stress-tests the load against weak pricing conditions: market rate pressure, scenario failure, deadhead exposure, thin margin and fuel sensitivity. A high score means the load needs negotiation, cost reduction, or rejection.

Risk Bands

0–39: Low
40–69: Medium
70–100: High
