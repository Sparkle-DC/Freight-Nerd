What market comparison tells you

Recommended RPM tells you what you need. Market RPM tells you what the lane may support. The gap between them is the negotiation problem. If required RPM is above market, the load may be structurally weak unless you can reduce cost or deadhead.

Market Logic

Required RPM ≤ Market RPM = viable
Required RPM > Market RPM = pressure
