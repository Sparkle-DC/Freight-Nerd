Why scenario simulation matters

A freight quote can look good at the base assumptions and fail after fuel moves, deadhead increases, or routing changes. Scenario simulation shows whether your quote still survives when reality gets worse.

Stress Logic

Scenario cost = base cost + fuel increase
Scenario RPM = scenario target revenue ÷ loaded miles
