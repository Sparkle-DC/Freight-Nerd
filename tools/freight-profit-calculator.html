How strategy mode works

Strategy mode changes the decision threshold. Safe mode protects margin and rejects fragile loads faster. Balanced mode uses normal discipline. Aggressive mode accepts more risk when winning volume or keeping a truck moving matters more than maximum margin.

Strategy Defaults

Safe: 25% margin / 10% cushion
Balanced: 20% margin / 5% cushion
Aggressive: 12% margin / 2% cushion
