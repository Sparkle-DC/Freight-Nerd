About FreightNerd

FreightNerd is a collection of free, no-signup logistics calculators and tools built for shippers, freight forwarders, owner-operators, and brokers.

Every tool is designed to solve a real shipping problem — from calculating CBM and freight class to estimating landed cost and comparing transit modes.

Built by someone who actually deals with freight. No fluff, no upsells — just tools that work.
