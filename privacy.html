Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FreightNerd does not collect or store any personal data on servers.

All calculations run entirely in your browser. No account is required. No usage data, analytics, or form submissions are sent to any backend server.

What data is stored locally

Some tools use your browser's localStorage to save data on your own device for convenience:

  • Notepad: Notes you type are saved locally and remain only on your device.
  • Freight Profit Calculator: Saved quotes are stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

This local data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to FreightNerd or any third party. You can delete it by clearing your browser's site data at any time.

Third-party resources

FreightNerd loads the following resources from third-party CDNs to render the interface:

  • Tailwind CSS — served from cdn.tailwindcss.com
  • Font Awesome — served from cdnjs.cloudflare.com
  • Google Fonts (Inter) — served from fonts.googleapis.com

Loading these resources may allow those providers to log your IP address per their own privacy policies. FreightNerd has no control over those logs.

Cookies

FreightNerd does not set any cookies. No tracking cookies, session cookies, or advertising cookies are used.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at hello@freightnerd.com.
