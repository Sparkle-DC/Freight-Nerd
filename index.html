What is FreightNerd?

FreightNerd is a free logistics calculator hub for quick shipping math. It is useful for freight brokers, importers, exporters, truckers, warehouse teams and small businesses comparing shipping options.

The tools run in your browser and are designed for fast estimates: CBM, cubic feet, LTL density, freight class planning, pallet fit, rate per mile, fuel surcharge, landed cost and profit checks.

Use FreightNerd for planning, not final compliance. Carrier quotes, customs duties, taxes, classifications and transit times should be verified before making commercial decisions.
